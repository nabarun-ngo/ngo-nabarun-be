About Us

We Help People In Need

NABARUN, is a Non-Profit Charitable social development society founded by a group of college students in the year 2018. We started our work in various sectors of the society. We are working in the development of social upliftment to support the underprivileged and marginalized people in rural areas where they are going through difficulties to survive. We are focused on one of the key responsibilities which is taking care of orphans, senior citizens, cancer patients, handicapped people who have been deprived by the economic and social systems of the society and providing proper care to the street animals.

I am delighted to welcome you to our community-driven non-governmental organization. Nabarun is not just an organization; it's a beacon of hope, dedicated to making a positive impact in the world.

Together, we strive to tackle pressing issues, uplift communities, and create lasting change. With your support and dedication, I am confident that we can achieve remarkable things.

Thank you for your interest on this meaningful journey. Let's work together to build a brighter future for all.

Mriganka Shekhar Pal,
Founder
